SSS Studio

Fill in your deck details, generate a prompt, hand it to any LLM, paste back what it gives you, and download a ready-to-teach slide deck. Covers two domains — MBA/EMBA and Software & Systems Engineering. Runs entirely in your browser — no API key, no backend, nothing sent anywhere. One requirement: open this page through its web address (the link you were given, or GitHub Pages), not by double-clicking the file on your computer — opened as a local file, the steps below won't load.

1Deck details

Not a cosmetic switch — each domain has its own voice, structure, and slide types built for that audience. Engineering scope is software/CS-flavored systems engineering (distributed systems, databases, networking, security, ML infra) — not embedded, electrical, or mechanical.

Each theme is a fixed 6-color palette. Part accent colors are semantic (Section 5/6 of the master prompt), so they aren't freely recolorable here.

2Generate the content prompt

Builds a ready-to-paste prompt with the full brief and structural rules baked in — hand it to Claude, ChatGPT, or any LLM of your choice.

3Paste the LLM's response

Paste the JSON the LLM returned. Nothing here leaves your browser.

4Preview & download

Only enabled once validation passes. Builds your finished deck as a single file, ready to download and open.
